Tasty Stir-Fried Rice

Description

Tasty Stir-Fried Rice that is quick and easy to make. This recipe uses basic houshold ingedients and it is also great for utilizing leftovers. Feel free change or add vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Rice (preferably leftover rice)
  • Oil (preferably coconut or sesame oil)
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Chillies
  • Spring Onion
  • Bell Pepper
  • Sriracha Sauce
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Chop vegetables
  2. Heat oil in wok or pan
  3. Add vegetables except spring onion
  4. Cook until onion is starting to turn brown
  5. Add Rice
  6. Add Sauces
  7. Stir until everything is mixed together
  8. Serve and add spring onion
